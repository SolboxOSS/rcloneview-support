# How to Use a Discount Coupon

:::info Discount Coupon Policy
A discount coupon is issued per user (email address) and can be applied regardless of purchase quantity. It can be used only once.
:::

Use the steps below to apply a discount coupon when you purchase a Plus or Lifetime license.

1. Save or copy the discount coupon code from the email you received.
2. Go to rcloneview.com and open [the Plus purchase page](https://rcloneview.com/src/pricing), then click **Buy Now** for either the Plus or Lifetime license.

<img src="/support/images/en/howto/FAQ/click-buy-now-buttons.png" alt="click buy now buttons" class="img-medium img-center" />

3. Enter the email address where you want to receive your license code, then click **Continue**.
<img src="/support/images/en/howto/FAQ/enter-email-address.png" alt="enter email address" class="img-medium img-center" />

4. Click `**Enter Coupon Code**`.

<img src="/support/images/en/howto/FAQ/click-enter-coupon-code.png" alt="click enter coupon code" class="img-medium img-center" />

5. Paste your saved discount coupon code, click **Apply**, confirm the discounted total appears, and complete the payment.

<img src="/support/images/en/howto/FAQ/enter-your-coupon-code.png" alt="enter your coupon code" class="img-medium img-center" />
